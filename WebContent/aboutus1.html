About Us

   About Us

“High - On - Code”

We often listen to stories of campus recruitments or have been one of the characters in these stories.

But what goes behind making these successful placement stories is the rigorous hard work and procedures that is put in by the recruiters, as well as the candidates who go through these tedious procedures.

Hence we came together and developed HUIT (HUnt for IT). HUIT acts as a one stop solution for both the recruiters as well as the candidates for a seamless and hassle free test conducting and test taking experience. Conduct a test, minus the papers and paperwork. Take a mock test before the live test. Select candidates and notify them in a click.

Let the HUnt for IT begin.
